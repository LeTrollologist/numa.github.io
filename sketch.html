Shortcuts & tips

View

  • Wheel zoom · Ctrl+Wheel faster · Space+drag pan
  • Ctrl+0 actual pixels (100%) · Ctrl+1 fit view

Tools

  • B brush · E eraser · F fill · L line · S spray
  • X swap colours · right-click paints with secondary colour
  • Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z undo / redo (all layers)
  • Clone: Alt+click source, then paint · toggle Aligned clone in the sidebar
  • Polygon: Enter to close path

Selection

  • Marquee, lasso, and wand create a selection; brushes and fills respect it.
  • Use feather / expand / contract in the right panel.

Export

  • Enable WebP or Alpha PNG before Export image. Layers ZIP needs JSZip (CDN or local jszip.min.js).
