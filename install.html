Chrome & Edge (PC/Mac)

  1. Click the glowing **"Install App"** button above.
  2. Alternatively, tap the **PWA Install Icon** in the right-hand corner of your browser's address bar.
  3. Select **"Install"** in the browser pop-up window.

Apple iPhone & iPad

  1. Open this page inside Apple's native **Safari Browser**.
  2. Tap the **Share Icon** in Safari's navigation toolbar.
  3. Scroll down the actions sheet and select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
  4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner.

Google Chrome for Android

  1. Click the glowing **"Install App"** button above.
  2. If it doesn't appear, tap the **Menu Icon** (three vertical dots) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  3. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home screen"** from the drop-down menu list.
